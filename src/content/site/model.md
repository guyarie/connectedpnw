---
title: "Coaching Model"
kicker: "Coaching model"
section_heading: "Learn → Practice → Give and receive feedback → Integrate → Ready for mature relationships"
section_intro: "This is the core coaching model behind Connected PNW. Participants do more than gain insight: they learn, practice in real interactions, receive feedback, integrate what they discover over time, and build the capacity for more mature, healthy relationships."
model_steps:
  - title: "Learn"
    body: "Understand patterns, defenses, communication style, and relational habits."
  - title: "Practice"
    body: "Try new skills in group exercises, Social Nights, and guided dating practice."
  - title: "Give & receive feedback"
    body: "Receive constructive reflection from facilitators and peers, and learn to offer it too."
  - title: "Integrate"
    body: "Apply what you are learning over time so it becomes part of how you actually relate."
  - title: "Ready for mature relationships"
    body: "Build clarity, confidence, and relational capacity for healthier partnership."
---
